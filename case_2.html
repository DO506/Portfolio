Project Overview

The product

Historical gallery is an mobile app that will help use to view and reate the olden or historical photo of bhutan .it also provide description of the photo.

Project duration

15th August 2022 to november 25th 2022

The problem

No available platform to go back to olden days.

The Goal

Goal is to preserve olden photos. And to make young generation know about olden days

Target Audience

History Lover, Old Photography lover

My role:

UX designer, UX researcher

Responsibilities

Conducting research and designing wireframes and prototype

❮ ❯

Understanding the user

User Research: Summary

I conducted interviews and created empathy maps to understand the users I’m designing for and their needs. Some to user group are people who are interested in learning history and reading history.

User research: pain points

1. No Platform: No proper platform.
2. No Detail: No detail history about picture on internet.
3. Time: Time consuming to visit museum
